01 — About

HR background. Builder's mindset.

My day job is HR — which means I've spent years buried in tracking sheets, headcount reports, onboarding checklists and requests that always start with "can you just put this in Excel." Instead of accepting that as the ceiling, I taught myself to build the tools that HR (and every other department) actually needs: dashboards that update themselves, sites that present data cleanly, and automations that remove the copy-paste.

That mix is what I bring to freelance work — I understand the operational side of a business, and I can build the software for it.
